The shift to online learning has transformed how students acquire financial skills.

## Key Trends
- **Gamified Learning:** Apps that use rewards to encourage consistent practice.
- **Microlearning Modules:** Short, focused lessons optimized for mobile.
- **AI-Powered Budgeting Tools:** Personalized recommendations for saving and investing.

## Opportunities
Integrating digital platforms into school curricula can make financial education more engaging and scalable.

## Conclusion
A hybrid approach, combining digital resources with in-person support, may offer the best outcomes.
